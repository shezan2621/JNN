The Chapter I Still Dream About

A Letter From The Future
♡

After everything we have lived through, there is one final chapter I want to share with you.

Not a chapter from our past.

A chapter from our future.

Sometimes I close my eyes and imagine a day that has not arrived yet.

The sun is slowly setting over Cox's Bazar.

The sky is painted with shades of orange and gold.

You and I are walking side by side along the shoreline, our hands gently holding each other. The waves reach our feet and then disappear again, as if the sea itself is quietly telling our story.

For a moment, everything feels peaceful.

Then suddenly, a little girl comes running towards us from a distance, laughing as she tries to reach us before the next wave arrives.

That little girl is not a stranger.

She is our daughter, Suzana.

As we stand there watching her play, my phone rings.

It is Amma.

In her familiar voice, she asks,

"When are you and Nila coming home?"

And I smile and reply,

"In Sha Allah, tomorrow."

It is such a simple conversation.

Yet somehow, it feels like one of the most beautiful moments I can imagine.

I often dream about the life that follows.

A home of our own.

Maybe not the biggest house in the world.

Maybe not the most expensive.

But warm.

Comfortable.

Filled with laughter.

Filled with memories.

And beautifully designed by the person who has always had better taste than me—you.

I imagine our mornings.

Waking up beside you.

Sharing breakfast together.

Beginning another ordinary day that somehow feels special simply because we are sharing it.

I imagine leaving for work after placing a kiss on your forehead and asking Allah to bless our day.

Then calling you after arriving and saying,

"Alhamdulillah, I reached safely."

I imagine coming home after a long day and finding you waiting at the door.

Just as you used to wait for me years ago.

Some things change with time.

Some things never do.

I imagine evenings spent together with Amma, Abba, you, me, and our little Suzana.

Planning small trips.

Sharing stories.

Laughing about ordinary things that somehow become extraordinary when shared with the people we love.

Most of all, I imagine the days themselves.

One day becoming another.

One page becoming another.

One memory becoming another.

And our story continuing to grow, chapter after chapter, year after year.

Because when I think about the future, I do not dream about wealth or success first.

I dream about moments.

The small moments.

The ordinary moments.

The moments that seem insignificant while they are happening.

Yet become the memories we treasure most years later.

Because those moments are the ones we get to share together.

And before I end this final chapter, there is only one thing left to say.

Whether it is the Nila from my memories,

The Nila who is part of my present,

Or the Nila I dream of spending my future with,

My answer will always remain the same.

I love you.

I loved you yesterday.

I love you today.

And In Sha Allah, I will continue loving you in every chapter that is still waiting to be written.

Happy Birthday, Nila.

And thank you for being my favorite chapter.

With all my love,

Shezan
